Print Making!

The world of blocks and ink!

Print making is currently my most prevalent hobby, as it is one that is easy to continue while on the go. I can take a block, or linoleum sheet, with me in my bag and continue to carve if I have down time during the day.

While making and carving blocks is fun and easy to bring along, actually printing the block is a different story. I usually use oil based ink, which tends to be messy and thick, meaning clean up can be extensive. Due to these issues, it's better to print in a spacious area that is well ventilated.
